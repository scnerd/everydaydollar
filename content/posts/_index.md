## Where to Start

New to The Easy Dollar? Start with the [Introduction](/posts/introduction/), then follow the [Outline](/outline/) to explore topics in order.

**The recommended path:**
1. **Personal Finances** - Learn budgeting, banking, insurance, and emergency funds first
2. **Investing Fundamentals** - Understand what investing is and how it works
3. **Retirement Accounts** - Maximize tax-advantaged investing
4. **Simple Portfolios** - The 1-fund or 3-fund approach
5. **Advanced Topics** - Dig deeper when you're ready
