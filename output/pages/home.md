Know what you want to do with your money, but not sure how to do it? Tired of fancy jargon and confusing advice? Scared of being conned by bad advice?

Never put your money into something you don't understand.

I'm not here to tell you what to do with your money.

I'm here to help you understand.

Then you'll be empowered to decide, for yourself, what's best _for you_.

If you're new to The Easy Dollar, [check out the Introduction](https://dollarsexplained.wordpress.com/2021/04/06/introduction/).
